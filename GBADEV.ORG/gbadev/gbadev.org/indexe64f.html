Game: Blast Arena Advance
By DuoDreamr [Wednesday July 27th 22:41:57 UTC 2005]		 News : General
Permalink
Matt Carr dropped me a line to let us know about a game he's just released called Blast Arena Advance.



I've written a retro-styled action game for the Game Boy Advance named
Blast Arena Advance. The objective of the game is to collect the
jittering yellow squares, while avoiding the shrapnel thrown out by
the explosions. You'll be rated on both score and time, and scores are
saved to cart.
